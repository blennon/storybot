Willow is a one year old girl who likes to explore and play with her toys. She has a lot of energy and is very curious. She also has a short attention span. Willow is also very stubborn and has a hard time listening to her parents. She has an older sister who is 3 years old. Willow lives in a small town in the middle of nowhere with her parents and older sister. The town is small and boring. Willow's parents want her to learn self-control, so they decide to tell her a story about a princess who learned self-control. The story teaches Willow about the importance of self-control. Willow learns that she can use self-control to do things like stay calm, focus on her tasks and resist temptation.
