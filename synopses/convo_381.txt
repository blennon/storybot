Emily is a very kind and caring four year old girl. She loves animals and always tries to help others, even if they don't always want her help. She's also very curious and loves to ask questions, especially about animals and bugs. Emily loves to learn and is always excited to go to school and learn new things.

One day, when Emily is out playing with her animal friends, she finds a hurt bird. She quickly runs to get help from her aunt. Together they take care of the bird and help it get better. Emily is very happy that she could help her new friend and is excited to continue helping animals in need.
