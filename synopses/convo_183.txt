Declan is a four-year-old boy who loves to have adventures. He is always trying new things, but sometimes he finds them too difficult and wants to give up. One day, he tries to do something that's really hard, but he just can't seem to succeed no matter how many times he tries. He gets frustrated and wants to give up, but his parents encourage him to keep trying. He eventually succeeds and learns that perseverance pays off. This story teaches young readers that it's important to never give up, even when things are tough.
