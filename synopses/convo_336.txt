Chloe tries to fix it herself but only makes it worse. She then tries to replace the vase with something else but her mom notices and she has to find a way to get the real vase back without her mom noticing. Chloe tries to sneak the vase back into her mom's house but her mom notices and she has to find a way to get the vase back without her mom noticing. Chloe tries to distract her mom with something else while she sneaks the vase back but her mom is always one step ahead.
