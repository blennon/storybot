In the story, one-year-old Luke is out with his family for a walk in the park. Suddenly, a big gust of wind comes and blows away all of the leaves from the trees. Luke's mom and dad start to clean up the leaves, but there are too many for them to handle. Luke sees a little girl crying because she can't find her mommy. He decides to help her and starts picking up leaves and putting them in a pile. After a while, he finds the little girl's mommy and she is so grateful. She gives Luke a big hug and a Thank You card. Luke is a hero and learns a valuable lesson about gratitude.
