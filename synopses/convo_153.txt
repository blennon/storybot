Declan is a 3 year old boy who is always wanting to help others. He's very compassionate and loves animals. One day, when Declan is out walking with his mom, they see a lost puppy on the side of the road. Declan's mom is hesitant to stop and help, but Declan is insistent. He begs and begs until his mom finally relents and they take the puppy home. From then on, Declan takes care of the puppy and they become best friends. He even starts to call the puppy his "compassion buddy." Through their friendship, Declan learns the importance of compassion and helping others. By the end of the story, the puppy has found a forever home with Declan and his family.
