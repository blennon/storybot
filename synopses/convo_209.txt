Declan is a happy and curious 1-year-old boy. He loves to explore his home and yard, and is always getting into mischief. One day, whileDeclan is playing in his backyard, he finds a strange object half-buried in the dirt. He starts to dig it out, and soon discovers that it's a broken toy robot.

Declan is fascinated by the robot, and he spends the rest of the day trying to fix it. With a little help from his grandpa, he eventually gets the robot working again. Now Declan has a new best friend, and he can't wait to show the robot all his favorite places.
