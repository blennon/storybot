Quinn is a spunky 3-year-old girl who loves to go on adventures with her friends. One day, they go on a hike and Quinn gets lost. She wanders around for a while until she comes across a cave. She goes inside and finds a group of friendly animals who invite her to stay with them. Quinn has a great time with her new friends, but she misses her family and friends. After a few days, she decides to go home. On her way back, she gets lost again but this time she is able to find her way home with the help of her new friends. She learns that even though she made a mistake, her family and friends still love her and forgive her.
