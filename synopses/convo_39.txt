Our story takes place in a backyard and follows the adventures of a young boy named Rowan, his sister Lila, and their dog Max. Rowan is a curious and active 2-year-old who loves to explore his surroundings. He is also very kind and always looks out for his little sister. In the story, Rowan goes on various adventures in the backyard with his sister and dog. Through these adventures, Rowan learns important lessons about kindness. In the end, Rowan learns that being kind is always the best choice and that it makes everyone happy.
