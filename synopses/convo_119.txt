The Little Engine That Could is a story about a small train engine that is tasked with pulling a large train over a mountain. The engine is unsure if it can do it, but with the help of its friends, it perseveres and succeeds.

The story follows Charlotte, the small engine, as she doubts her ability to pull the large train. Her friends, including a big engine and a helpful mouse, encourage her to try her best and never give up. Charlotte eventually succeeds in pulling the train over the mountain, proving to herself and her friends that she is capable of anything.
