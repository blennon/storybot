Evelyn is a 1 year old girl who is very curious and loves to explore. She's also very kind. One day, Evelyn's mommy finds her exploring in the backyard. Evelyn has found a caterpillar and is very curious about it. Her mommy tells her that the caterpillar is going to turn into a butterfly. Evelyn is amazed and can't wait to see the butterfly.

A few days later, Evelyn's mommy takes her outside to show her the butterfly. Evelyn is so excited to see it! The butterfly is beautiful and Evelyn is very happy. She learns that being kind and helping others can be very rewarding.
