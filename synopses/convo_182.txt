Rowan is a 3-year-old boy who lives in medieval times. One day, he finds a dragon in his backyard and befriends it. However, when the dragon accidentally burns down Rowan's house, Rowan's parents are very angry and banish the dragon from their property. Rowan is very sad and misses his friend, but he knows that he needs to forgive the dragon in order to move on. With the help of his parents, Rowan is able to forgive the dragon and they become friends again.
