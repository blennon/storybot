Mateo is a four year old boy who starts school. He is excited to make new friends, but soon discovers that not everyone is as kind as they seem. Mateo is targeted by a bully, and starts to feel alone and afraid.

One day, Mateo musters up the courage to stand up to his bully. He stands up for himself, and for the other kids who have been targeted. Mateo learns that compassion and kindness are the best weapons against bullies. He also learns that he is not alone, and that he has the power to make a difference in the world.
