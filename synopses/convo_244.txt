Piper is a curious and active two-year-old who loves to explore her backyard with her friends. One day, Piper and her friends find a magical kingdom that they had never seen before. They explore the kingdom and meet the friendly inhabitants who teach them about the importance of being kind to others. Piper and her friends have many adventures in the kingdom and learn valuable lessons along the way. In the end, they return to their own backyard, where they share their experiences with their families and friends.
