Ryder is a 4 year old boy who loves to play and explore. He's always asking questions about the world around him. Ryder is afraid of the dark, but he's a brave boy who is always looking for new adventures. One day, Ryder goes on an adventure to learn about courage. He learns that courage is not being afraid to try new things and to face his fears. With the help of his friends, Ryder overcomes his fear of the dark and learns to be a brave boy.
