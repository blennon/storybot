Owen is a 3-year-old boy who lives in a future world where everyone has special abilities. One day, he meets a girl named Lila who doesn't have any powers. Owen is immediately drawn to her and wants to be her friend.

Despite the fact that Lila is different, Owen is determined to include her in his adventures. He uses his own powers to help her, and together they discover that even though she doesn't have any superpowers, she is still special and has a lot to offer.

In the end, Owen learns an important lesson about compassion: that it isn't about having abilities, it's about being kind and caring towards others.
