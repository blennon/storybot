Luke, Timmy and Sarah are three 3-year-old best friends who love to play together at the park. One day, they see a new boy playing by himself on the swings. His name is Billy, and he looks sad. The three friends approach him and ask him to play with them. Billy is hesitant at first, but he eventually agrees.

The four children have a lot of fun playing together. They swing on the swings, play tag, and climb on the jungle gym. Billy begins to smile and laugh.

At the end of the day, it's time to go home. Luke, Timmy and Sarah invite Billy to come and play with them again tomorrow. Billy is happy and says yes.

The story teaches about the importance of kindness and making new friends.
