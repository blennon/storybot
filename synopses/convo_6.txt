Rowan is a two year old boy who loves to play with his toys. He has a few friends that he likes to play with, but his best friend is his stuffed animal, Mr. Snuggles. Rowan is a very mischievous little boy and loves to play tricks on his family and friends. He is very close to his grandparents. They love spending time with him and he always make them laugh. In the story, Rowan gets into some mischief and has to learn to forgive his friends. The story is a comedy with the lesson of forgiveness.
