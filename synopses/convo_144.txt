Willow is a 3-year-old girl who loves to explore and is very curious. She's also very determined and once she sets her mind to something, she'll do whatever it takes to achieve it. Willow is very active and loves to be on the move, always running and climbing. She's also very impulsive and doesn't always think things through before she acts. As a result, she's often emotional and gets upset easily. In this story, Willow learns to control her impulses and emotions. She learns to think things through before she acts and to not let her emotions get the best of her. As a result, she becomes a happier and more well-rounded child.
