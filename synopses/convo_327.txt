Ryder is a four year old boy who loves dinosaurs. When he learns that he can travel back in time to meet them, he can't wait to go on his adventure.

During his travels, Ryder learns about the importance of self-control. He meets many different dinosaurs, some of which are friendly and some of which are not. He quickly learns that he needs to be careful and use his self-control in order to stay safe.

In the end, Ryder returns to the present day having had a wonderful adventure. He is excited to tell his family and friends all about what he has learned.
