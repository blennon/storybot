Mateo is a 3 year old boy who lives in a small village with his parents and grandparents. One day, Mateo finds a mysterious egg in the forest and decides to take it home. When the egg hatches, a baby dragon is born! Mateo names him Spike and the two become best friends.

One day, a group of hunters comes to the village, looking for dragons. When they find out about Spike, they kidnap him and take him away to sell him. Mateo is heartbroken and sets out to find his friend.

With the help of some friendly animals, Mateo finally finds the hunters' camp. He comes up with a plan to rescue Spike and, with a little bit of perseverance, he is successful! Mateo and Spike are reunited and they live happily ever after.
