Luke is a two-year-old boy who is very active and loves to explore. He's also very kind-hearted. His best friend is a dragon who is always causing trouble. One day, the dragon eats all the food and Luke has to find a way to get some for himself. He tricks the dragon into giving him some of the food and then shares it with the dragon. The dragon is so grateful that it starts helping Luke on his adventures. They go on a treasure hunt and find a secret passage that leads to a hidden treasure.
