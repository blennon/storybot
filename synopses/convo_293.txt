Elijah is a happy, curious three-year-old boy who loves exploring the world around him. When his family goes on a safari, Elijah is excited to see all the new animals and places. But he soon discovers that some things on the safari are a bit scary, like the dark and some of the animals.

Elijah is scared at first, but he learns that he can be brave in the face of new challenges. He overcomes his fears and has a great time on the safari, learning about all the different animals and seeing new places. In the end, he learns that there's nothing to be afraid of when you're brave.
