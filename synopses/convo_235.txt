In a magical kingdom far, far away, there lives a young boy named Rowan. Rowan is a happy boy, but he often takes things for granted and doesn't always show gratitude for what he has. One day, Rowan's kingdom is threatened by a dark force. In order to protect his people, Rowan must go on a quest to find a magical relic that will vanquish the darkness. Along the way, Rowan learns many lessons about gratitude and learns to appreciate all the good in his life. In the end, Rowan triumphs over the darkness and is hailed as a hero by his people.
