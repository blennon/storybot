Aidan is a one year old boy who lives in a house with his grandparents. One day, a small, pesky animal comes into the house and starts causing trouble. Aidan's grandfather tries to catch the animal, but it's very quick and always manages to stay one step ahead. This goes on for several days, with the animal wreaking havoc throughout the house and Aidan's grandfather getting more and more frustrated. Finally, Aidan's grandfather manages to catch the animal, but it turns out to be a very small and harmless mouse. Aidan's grandfather has learned a valuable lesson in patience, and Aidan has learned a valuable lesson in comedy.
