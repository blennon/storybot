Owen is a 3-year-old boy who lives in the city with his parents. One day, Owen's parents take him to visit the forest. Owen has never been to the forest before and he is a little bit scared. When they arrive, Owen meets a friendly deer named Bambi. Bambi helps Owen overcome his fears and learn about courage. Owen learns that it's okay to be scared, but he should still be brave and face his fears. In the end, Owen is brave and he learns a valuable lesson about courage.
