Landon is a four-year-old boy who loves to explore. He's always getting into mischief, much to the dismay of his parents. One day, while Landon is out exploring in the woods, he comes across a magical cave. He enters the cave and finds himself in a strange and wonderful world. Landon meets some friendly creatures who live in this world and soon discovers that he has the power to do magic. He has many adventures in this magical world and eventually learns that he needs to use his powers wisely.
