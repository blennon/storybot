Kai is a spirited two-year-old boy who is always into something. He has a bit of a temper, but is ultimately a good kid. Kai is trying to break the habit of biting his nails, but finds it difficult to resist. His parents and daycare providers help him to find alternative activities to do with his hands and mouth when he feels the urge to bite his nails. Through patience and practice, Kai eventually succeeds in breaking his bad habit.
