Aidan is a curious two-year-old boy who loves learning new things. One day, he sets his sights on learning how to ride a bike. Aidan's parents try to teach him, but he struggles at first and gets frustrated. He doesn't give up though, and with a little bit of practice and perseverance, he finally learns how to ride a bike. Aidan is thrilled with his accomplishment and can't wait to show off his new skills to his friends and family.
