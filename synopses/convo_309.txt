Abigail is a four-year-old girl who is very impatient. She is always rushing through things and never takes the time to enjoy the moment. One day, she gets a magical stopwatch from a mysterious stranger. The stopwatch has the power to stop time for everyone but Abigail. She is thrilled with her new toy and starts using it to have fun. She runs around the park, stopping time to play with the animals. She even uses it to get out of going to bed on time. But when she accidentally leaves the stopwatch on and her mom and dad get frozen in time, she realizes that she needs to be more careful with her new power. Abigail learns to use the stopwatch responsibly and discovers the joy of taking her time.
