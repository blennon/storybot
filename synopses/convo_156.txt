One year old Declan is playing in his backyard when he finds a lost puppy. He takes the puppy in and nurses it back to health. When the puppy is better, Declan's family decides to keep him and they name him Simon. From then on, Declan and Simon are best friends. They go on all kinds of adventures together, learning about different things along the way. One day, they meet a sick cat and help her get better. They become friends with the cat and name her Mia. Now Declan, Simon, and Mia are the best of friends and go on even more adventures together, always learning and growing along the way.
