Quinn is a 3-year-old girl who loves to play with her toys and run around outside. She has a few friends from her daycare, and they all love to play together. One day, they discover a magical kingdom that they can enter by finding a secret doorway in the forest. They meet the king and queen of the kingdom, who are very kind and welcoming. The children explore the kingdom and have all sorts of fun adventures. They also learn about the importance of being kind to others. In the end, they all return to their own homes, but they keep in touch and remain good friends.
