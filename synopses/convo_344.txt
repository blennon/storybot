"Paige and the Perseverance Test" is a comedy about a 2 year old girl named Paige who has to overcome various obstacles throughout her day. The story teaches about the importance of perseverance and how to overcome adversity. Paige is faced with several challenges, including learning how to use the potty, eating her vegetables, and taking a nap. Through her determination and stubbornness, Paige is able to overcome each challenge and learns an important lesson in the process.
