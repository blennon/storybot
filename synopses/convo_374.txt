Ryder is a 1 year old male protagonist who is very active and loves to explore. He is always into something. One day, Ryder's mother goes to work and leaves him with a mean babysitter who doesn't let him play outside. Ryder gets bored and decides to explore the house. He finds a key and unlocks the door to the backyard. He starts to play in the sand when he sees a spaceship. He gets in and starts to fly away. The babysitter tries to stop him, but he is too fast. Ryder flies to different planets and has adventures. He meets new friends and learns about different cultures. In the end, Ryder returns home and the babysitter learns a lesson about being kind to others.
