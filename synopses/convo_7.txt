Aidan is a one year old boy who lives in a small town in the United States. He is a very happy and curious child who loves to explore and is always getting into mischief. He is also very kind and always looks out for others, even though he is still so young.

One day, Aidan gets into some trouble when he tries to help a lost puppy find its way home. His parents scold him and tell him to stay out of trouble. But Aidan doesn't listen and he continues to help the puppy, even though it means getting into more trouble.

In the end, Aidan's kind heart and helpful nature are rewarded when the puppy is safely returned to its owner and Aidan learns a valuable lesson about kindness.
