Owen is a four year old boy who lives on a farm with his parents. One day, while he is playing outside, he hears a puppy crying. He follows the sound and finds a lost puppy who is scared and alone. Owen decides to help the puppy and takes it home. His parents are not happy to see the puppy, but Owen is determined to keep it and care for it. He names the puppy Lucky and they become best friends. Through caring for Lucky, Owen learns about compassion and the importance of helping others.
