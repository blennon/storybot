Once upon a time, there was a young princess named Savannah who was always ungrateful for what she had. She complained about her toys, her clothes and anything else she could think of. One day, a fairy godmother grants her a wish to show her how bad it could be if she doesn't learn to be thankful. The princess is then transported to a land where she has nothing and is forced to live a life of poverty and misery. She soon realizes how lucky she is to have what she does and learns the importance of being thankful. In the end, she is transported back to her kingdom and lives happily ever after, having learned her lesson.
