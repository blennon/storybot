Declan is a four year old boy who is the protagonist of the story. The story is a comedy that teaches about perseverance. The story begins with Declan waking up from a nap and being very hungry. He goes to the kitchen to find that there is no food. He looks in the fridge, the cupboards, and even under the sink, but he can't find anything to eat. He is about to give up when he hears his mom coming home. He knows that if he can just find something to eat before she comes in, she will be so proud of him. He looks one last time and finds a cookie. He eats it and is very happy. Just then, his mom comes in and sees the empty cookie jar. She is so proud of her son for finding something to eat and teaches him about perseverance.
