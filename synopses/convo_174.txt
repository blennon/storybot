Harper is a 2 year old girl who loves to explore. She's very active and loves to discover new things. Harper is also very compassionate. She's always quick to comfort her friends when they're sad or upset.

One day, Harper's friend Sarah comes to her crying. Sarah's cat, Snuggles, has run away and she's very upset. Harper is determined to help find Snuggles. She looks all over the neighborhood, asking everyone she sees if they've seen the missing cat.

Finally, Harper finds Snuggles hiding in a tree. She climbs up to get him down and returns him safely to Sarah. Sarah is so grateful to Harper for her help. This experience teaches Harper the importance of compassion and helping others.
