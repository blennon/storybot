Declan is a 3 year old boy who lives in the country. He is a very friendly child who loves to make new friends. One day, he meets a new friend named Benny. Benny is a very shy child who doesn't talk to anyone. Declan is determined to make Benny feel comfortable and to be his friend. He talks to Benny and helps him to open up. Benny finally starts to talk to Declan and they become fast friends. The story teaches a lesson about compassion and how even the shyest child can make a new friend if they try.
