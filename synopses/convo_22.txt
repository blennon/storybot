Quinn is a 3 year old girl who loves space travel. She dreams of going to the moon and walking on its surface. One day, she gets the chance to travel to space with her family. But when they arrive at their destination, they find that there is no air on the moon! Quinn is brave and determined to find a way to breathe on the moon. With the help of her family, she eventually finds a way to breathe and fulfill her dream of walking on the moon.
