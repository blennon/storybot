Hayes is a two-year-old boy who loves to explore. He is always getting into mischief, much to the dismay of his parents. One day, Hayes decides to go on an adventure in the woods near his house. He gets lost and is unable to find his way back home. A kind-hearted rabbit takes Hayes under his wing and helps him find his way back home. Along the way, the two of them encounter many obstacles, but they eventually make it back safely. Hayes learns a valuable lesson about following directions and listening to his parents.
