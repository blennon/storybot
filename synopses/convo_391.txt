Violet is a one year old girl who loves to explore. She's curious about everything and loves to learn new things. When her family takes a trip to space, she's excited to see all the new things she can discover.

While in space, Violet learns that her family is in danger. They are being chased by a group of aliens who are trying to capture them. Violet is scared, but she knows she has to be brave. With the help of her family, she learns to be brave and they all escape from the aliens.

In the end, Violet learns that she can be brave even when she's scared. She also learns that her family will always be there for her.
