Sofia is a four year old girl who loves to explore and is very brave. She lives in the countryside with her grandparents. One day, she goes on an adventure into the woods near her house. She finds a magical door that leads her into a different world.

In this new world, Sofia meets all sorts of magical creatures. She also has all sorts of adventures, learning about courage along the way. In the end, Sofia returns home safely and is very happy. Her grandparents are also very happy to see her.
