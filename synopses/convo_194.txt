Charlotte is a four year old girl who is very brave and curious. She loves animals, and often goes on adventures with her pet dog, Benny. One day, Charlotte and Benny discover a secret garden that nobody knows about. They meet a friendly rabbit who tells them about all the different animals who live in the garden. Charlotte and Benny have lots of fun exploring the garden and making new friends. Eventually, they have to say goodbye to the garden and all their new friends, but they promise to come back soon.
