Owen is a 1 year old boy who loves to explore and ask questions. He's also very stubborn and doesn't like to listen to anyone. In the story, he gets curious about a strange noise he hears outside and goes to investigate. He finds a spaceship and climbs inside. The ship takes off and Owen is soon lost in space. He has to find a way to get back home. Along the way, he learns about perseverance and how to never give up. In the end, he finally makes it back home and is excited to tell his family about his adventures.
