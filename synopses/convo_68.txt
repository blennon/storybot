Aidan is a young boy who lives with his parents on a peaceful planet. One day, their idyllic life is shattered when Aidan's parents are taken away by aliens. Aidan is left alone, feeling betrayed and abandoned.

Aidan eventually learns that his parents were taken to help another planet that is in danger. He also learns that they did it willingly, knowing that they might not come back. Aidan is able to forgive them for leaving him and is proud of them for their selfless act.

The story ends with Aidan's parents being returned to him and the family being reunited.
