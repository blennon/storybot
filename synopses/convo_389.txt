Once upon a time, there was a brave knight named Max who lived in a kingdom by the sea. Max was always exploring and asking a lot of questions, and he was also very brave. One day, Max found out that there was a dragon in the kingdom who was terrorizing the people. Max was determined to stop the dragon and protect his kingdom. He rode his horse to the dragon's lair and confronted the dragon. The dragon was no match for Max's bravery and was soon defeated. The people of the kingdom were very grateful to Max and he became known as the bravest knight in the land.
