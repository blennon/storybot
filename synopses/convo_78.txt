Quinn is a one year old girl who loves to explore. She's curious about everything, and loves to ask questions. Her family is very important to her, and she loves spending time with them.

One day, Quinn's family goes on a space trip. Quinn is excited to explore the stars and planets. During their travels, they run into some trouble and are forced to land on an unknown planet.

While exploring the planet, they meet a friendly alien who helps them fix their ship. Quinn learns about the importance of forgiveness from the alien, and they all become fast friends.

In the end, Quinn and her family safely return home and she learns a valuable lesson about forgiveness.
