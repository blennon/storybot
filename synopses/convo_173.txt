Owen is a 3 year old boy who lives with his parents. They are all very kind to each other and always try to help out when someone is in need. One day, Owen's parents tell him that it is important to always be kind to others. They explain that being kind can make someone's day and make them feel better. Owen decides that he wants to be kind to everyone he meets. He starts by helping his neighbor carry groceries to her car. Then he helps a little old lady cross the street. He even gives his lunch to a hungry dog. Everyone is so surprised by Owen's acts of kindness and they all start to do the same. Soon the whole town is filled with kind people!
