Charlotte is a 3-year-old girl who loves to play outside. She's very active and loves to explore. One day, while she is playing in her backyard, she finds a shiny penny. She takes it inside to show her mom. Her mom asks her where she found it. Charlotte lies and says she found it in the living room. Her mom knows she is lying and asks her again where she found it. This time, Charlotte tells the truth and says she found it in the backyard. Her mom is proud of her for being honest and they share a hug.
