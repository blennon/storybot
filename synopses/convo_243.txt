Piper is a curious four year old girl who loves to explore. She and her little sister live in a small town in the middle of a forest. One day, while they are out exploring, they find a magic stone. When Piper picks it up, she is transported to a magical kingdom.

Piper meets a lot of new friends in the kingdom, but she is also faced with some challenges. She has to help her new friends solve some problems and stop the evil queen from taking over the kingdom. Along the way, Piper learns about gratitude and how important it is. She also learns that even when things are tough, she can overcome them with the help of her friends.

In the end, Piper and her friends are able to stop the evil queen and Piper is able to return home. She is grateful for her new friends and for the lessons she has learned.
