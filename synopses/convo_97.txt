In Harper's story, she faces the problem of being scared of the dark. When night falls, Harper becomes afraid of all the shadows in her room. She tries to stay up as long as she can to avoid going to bed, but eventually she gets tired and has to go to sleep. In the middle of the night, Harper wakes up and realizes that she's not scared anymore. She gets out of bed and turns on the light, and sees that there's nothing to be afraid of. Harper is happy to have overcome her fear and can go back to bed and sleep peacefully.
