Charlotte is a 4-year-old girl who is very active and curious. She loves to explore her surroundings and is always asking questions. One day, while she is playing in her backyard, she finds a mysterious object. She picks it up and starts to examine it. Suddenly, a door opens and she is transported to a different world.

In this new world, Charlotte meets some friendly creatures who tell her about the different places she can explore. She is also given a map to help her find her way around. Charlotte is excited to start exploring this new world and can't wait to see what else is in store for her.
