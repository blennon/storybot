Piper is a two year old girl who is sweet and curious. She loves animals and nature. One day, she goes for a walk in the forest with her grandmother. They see a mean witch who wants to hurt Piper. Piper's grandmother protects her and they run away. The witch is angry and casts a spell on them. They are both turned into animals. Piper is a rabbit and her grandmother is a bird. They live in the forest and try to avoid the witch. One day, they meet a friendly fox who helps them break the witch's spell. They are turned back into humans and they all live happily ever after.
