Aidan is a brave little boy who loves to explore. One day, he stumbles upon a secret space ship and decides to take it for a spin. Aidan has a blast flying the ship around his neighborhood, but when he accidentally crashes it into a tree, he knows he needs to tell his grandfather the truth. Even though he is afraid he will be in trouble, Aidan owns up to what he did and accepts responsibility for his actions. In the end, he learns that honesty is the best policy and is rewarded with a new space ship of his own.
