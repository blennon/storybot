Mateo is a four-year-old boy who loves to explore. He is always curious and loves to try new things. One day, while Mateo is out exploring, he comes across a cave. He has never seen a cave before and is curious to see what is inside. He climbs up the cave to take a look. Once he is inside, he realizes that there are bats inside the cave. He is scared at first, but then he starts to think about how cool it is to see bats up close. He decides to stay and watch them for a while. After a while, he gets tired and decides to head home. He is excited to tell his family and friends about his adventure.
