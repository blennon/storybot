Xavier is a four-year-old boy who loves to explore. He's always getting into mischief, much to the dismay of his parents and grandparents. One day, Xavier finds a mysterious box in the attic. He opens it up and discovers a magical kingdom inside. With the help of his new friends, Xavier goes on all sorts of adventures. He eventually learns the importance of responsibility and helping others. In the end, Xavier is crowned the new king of the kingdom and everyone lives happily ever after.
