Isabella is a 3 year old girl who is very active and loves to play outside. She is also very curious and loves to learn new things. One day, she finds a magic lamp and rubs it. A genie comes out and grants her three wishes. Isabella uses her first wish to have a never ending supply of candy. She is very happy and eats candy all day long. However, after a while she gets sick of candy and realizes that she took her second wish for granted. She then uses her third wish to wish for a more balanced life. The story ends with Isabella learning the importance of gratitude and being more mindful of what she wishes for.
