Charlotte is a four year old girl who loves to explore. She lives with her parents in a small town near a forest. One day, while Charlotte is playing near the forest, she finds a small, injured bird. Charlotte takes the bird home and nurses it back to health. She names the bird Twinkle.

As Charlotte and Twinkle become friends, they go on many adventures together. One day, they discover a secret path in the forest that leads to a magical place. They meet a friendly witch who lives in the magical place and who helps them with their adventures.

The witch teaches Charlotte and Twinkle how to do magic. They have many fun adventures together, using their magic to help people in their town. In the end, Charlotte and Twinkle are able to help the witch with a problem she's been having and they all live happily ever after.
