Elizabeth is a four year old girl with blonde hair and blue eyes. She is playing with her friend, Sarah, when she accidentally knocks over a block tower that Sarah has been working on. Sarah starts to cry and Elizabeth feels terrible. She doesn't know how to make it better.

Elizabeth's mom comes over and talks to Sarah. She helps Elizabeth understand that everyone makes mistakes and that it's important to forgive yourself. Elizabeth is also very grateful to Sarah for forgiving her.

In the end, Elizabeth and Sarah are both happy and they have learned some important lessons.
