Willow is a two-year-old girl who is very adventurous. She loves to explore and is always up for a new adventure. One day, Willow sets out to explore the world beyond her garden. She finds a path that leads her to a pirate ship. The ship is full of treasure and Willow is excited to find it. However, the ship is also full of dangerous pirates. Willow must be brave and use her wits to find a way to escape the ship and get back to her garden safely. Along the way, she learns an important lesson about honesty.
