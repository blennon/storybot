Max is a 1 year old boy who is brave and always stands up for what he believes in. He is also very curious and loves to explore. One day, while he is exploring his neighborhood, he comes across a group of kids who are picking on a smaller child. Max immediately stands up for the smaller child and tells the group of kids to stop. The group of kids doesn't listen to Max and continues to pick on the smaller child. Max gets angry and starts to cry. The group of kids sees that they are upsetting Max and they stop picking on the smaller child. Max is happy that he was able to stand up for what he believes in and help the smaller child.
