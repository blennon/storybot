Willow is a two-year-old girl who is very curious and loves to explore. She's also very independent and doesn't like being told what to do. One day, Willow goes exploring in the forest and gets lost. She meets a friendly bear who helps her find her way home. Along the way, Willow learns about the importance of forgiveness. She also learns that it's okay to ask for help when she's lost. When she finally gets home, she learns that her family was worried about her and that they're never going to let her out of their sight again.
