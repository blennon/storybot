Grayson is a young boy who loves to have fun. He loves to run around and play with his friends, but he doesn't always know when to stop. One day, Grayson's friends challenge him to see who can run the fastest. Grayson takes off without thinking and doesn't stop until he's out of breath. His friends laugh at him and tell him he's not very fast. Grayson is embarrassed and frustrated. He decides he needs to learn how to control himself better. With the help of his friends, Grayson learns how to pace himself and to know when to stop. He also learns that it's okay to lose sometimes. In the end, Grayson is a better runner and a better friend.
