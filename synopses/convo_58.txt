Owen is a 4 year old boy with blonde hair and blue eyes. He is active and loves to play, but can also be mischievous at times. In this story, Owen learns the importance of patience.

One day, Owen's parents tell him that he can't go outside to play until he finishes his lunch. Owen doesn't want to eat his lunch, so he tries to sneak out of the house. He gets caught by his mom and is sent back to the table.

Owen's mom tells him that he can't go outside until he finishes his lunch, and if he is patient, she will play with him when he is done. Owen doesn't want to wait, but he knows that if he is patient, he will get to play with his mom.

Owen finishes his lunch and goes outside to play with his mom. He learns that being patient can be rewarding.
