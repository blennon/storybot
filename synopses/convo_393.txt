Luna is a two-year-old girl who is happy and curious. She loves to learn new things, and she is always asking questions. One day, Luna sees a friend who is sad and crying. She doesn't know what to do, but she knows she wants to help. Luna goes up to her friend and gives her a hug. She then asks what is wrong. The friend tells Luna that she is being bullied by another girl in the class. Luna is horrified and decides to stand up for her friend. She goes to the bully and tells her to stop. When the bully doesn't listen, Luna punches her in the nose. The bully is surprised and stops bullying Luna's friend. Luna is a hero, and she learns a lesson about compassion.
