Claire is a two-year-old girl who lives on a space station with her family. She is very bright and curious, and loves learning new things. One day, she decides to explore the different planets that the space station is orbiting. She takes a spaceship to the first planet, which is inhabited by friendly aliens. They show her around and teach her about their culture. The next day, she visits a planet that is inhabited by an alien creature that is afraid of humans. However, Claire is kind to the creature and helps it to overcome its fear. In the end, the creature learns to trust humans and they all become friends.
