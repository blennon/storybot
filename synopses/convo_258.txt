Oliver is a one-year-old boy who loves to explore. He's always getting into mischief, but he means well. One day, Oliver's parents take him on a hike. He gets tired and wants to go home, but his parents tell him to be grateful for what he has and to keep going. Oliver doesn't understand and throws a tantrum. Suddenly, a giant bear appears! Oliver's parents are scared, but Oliver isn't. He approaches the bear and starts to play with it. The bear is actually really friendly and soon Oliver's parents are playing with the bear too. They all walk back home together and Oliver learns a valuable lesson about gratitude.
