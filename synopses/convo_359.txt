Elijah is a 3 year old boy who loves to explore. He's curious and adventurous, and loves to discover new things. One day, while he is exploring his backyard, he finds a hole in the fence. He crawls through it and ends up in his neighbor's yard. He starts to play with their dog, but the dog gets loose and runs away. Elijah's neighbor is very angry, and Elijah is very sorry. He learns that it's okay to make mistakes, and that everyone makes them. He also learns the importance of forgiveness, and that it's never too late to say sorry.
