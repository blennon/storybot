Rowan is a 3 year old boy who lives in a small town in the country. He is a kind boy who likes to help others. One day, Rowan's mom gives him a new toy to play with. Rowan is so excited to have the toy, but he soon realizes that he has to share it with his little sister. Rowan doesn't want to share his toy, but he knows it's the right thing to do. Rowan's mom helps him learn to share by giving him a treat every time he shares his toy. Rowan is happy to have learned such an important lesson.
