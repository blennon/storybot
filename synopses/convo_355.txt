Grayson is a two-year-old boy who loves to explore and is always up for an adventure. One day, he decides to wander off from his home in the city and ends up in the woods. He gets lost and is scared, but he soon meets a friendly bear who helps him find his way back home. Along the way, Grayson learns the importance of being kind and helping others. He also learns that even though he's just a little boy, he's capable of doing big things. When he finally makes it back home, he is greeted with a big hug from his relieved parents. Grayson is happy to be home and can't wait to go on his next adventure.
