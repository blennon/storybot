Declan is a 4 year old boy who loves space. He dreams of being an astronaut and exploring the galaxy. One day, he gets his wish and is blasted off into space on a top secret mission. Declan is joined by a team of brave astronauts and they begin their journey into the unknown. They face many challenges along the way, but with patience and perseverance they eventually reach their destination. They discover a new planet that is teeming with life. Declan is the first human to set foot on this new world and he is hailed as a hero. The moral of the story is that anything is possible if you are patient and never give up on your dreams.
