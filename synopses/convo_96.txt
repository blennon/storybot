Luke is a 4 year old boy who loves to explore and learn new things. He's always up for an adventure, no matter what it may be. One day, Luke's curiosity gets the best of him and he finds himself in a situation that is a bit too much for him to handle. He's scared and doesn't know what to do. But with a little bit of courage, Luke is able to overcome his fears and learn from his mistake. In the end, he's a better explorer for it.
