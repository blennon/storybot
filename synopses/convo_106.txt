Quinn is a 4 year old girl who loves to play at the park. She loves to swing on the swings, slide down the slide and play games with her friends. One day, while she is playing at the park, she sees a big, black dog. She has never seen a dog like this before and she is curious. She approaches the dog and starts to pet it. The dog is friendly and they become fast friends. Quinn and the dog play together at the park every day. One day, when Quinn is playing with the dog, she sees a rabbit and she runs over to pet it. The rabbit is scared and runs away. Quinn is sad that she scared the rabbit away and she decides to go home. On her way home, she sees the rabbit again. This time, she is very careful and she slowly approaches the rabbit. She pets the rabbit gently and the rabbit stays. Quinn is happy that she made a new friend.
