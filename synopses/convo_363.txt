Oliver is a one-year-old boy who loves to explore. He is curious and adventurous, always seeking out new things to discover. One day, he finds a mysterious box in his backyard. When he opens it, he is transported to a faraway planet. Oliver meets some friendly aliens who show him around. He has lots of fun learning about their culture and playing games with them. But when Oliver tries to go home, he finds himself in danger. He must use his wit and courage to find his way back to Earth. With the help of his new friends, Oliver finally makes it home safe and sound.
