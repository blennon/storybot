Max is a one year old boy who lives in space with his family. His parents are astronauts and his grandparents are scientists. One day, Max's grandparents take him on a tour of the space station they live on. While they are showing him around, Max accidentally floats away from them. He is captured by a group of aliens who are planning to sell him to a zoo on a nearby planet. Max's family is notified of his disappearance and they set out to rescue him. They reach the planet just in time and are able to save Max from being sold. The family is then able to return to their space station safely.
