Mateo is a two-year-old boy who loves to explore. He's always on the go, and his parents have a hard time keeping up with him. One day, Mateo's parents take him to the park. Mateo sees a bird and starts to chase it. His parents tell him to stop, but Mateo doesn't listen. He runs into the street and is almost hit by a car. Mateo's parents are very angry with him. They scold him and tell him he needs to learn to listen and be more careful. Mateo is very sorry for what he did. He promises to be more careful from now on.
