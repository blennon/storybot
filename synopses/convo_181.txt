Charlotte is a brave and courageous little girl who loves to explore. When she stumbles upon a magical world that is full of adventure, she does not hesitate to jump right in. Charlotte quickly learns that in order to make it back home, she must first face a big challenge. With the help of her new magical friends, Charlotte sets out to overcome the obstacles in her way. Along the way, she learns about courage and bravery, and ultimately discovers the true meaning of home.
