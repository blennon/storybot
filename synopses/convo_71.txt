Willow is a 4 year old girl who is very curious and loves to explore. She's also very brave and always looks out for others, even if they're not her friends. One day, Willow discovers a secret door in her house that leads to a fantasy world. She meets some friendly creatures in this world and together, they go on some exciting adventures. Through these adventures, Willow learns about gratitude and the importance of always being kind to others.
