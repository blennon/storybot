Aidan is a one year old boy who loves to explore. One day, he finds a strange object in his backyard. It's a spaceship, and inside is an alien! The alien is friendly, but he is also very messy. He makes a huge mess of Aidan's house, and Aidan's parents are not happy.

The alien needs to get back to his home planet, but he doesn't know how. Aidan decides to help him, and he learns that honesty is the best policy. With Aidan's help, the alien is able to get back to his home planet.
