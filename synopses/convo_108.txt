Piper is a curious two-year-old girl who loves to explore and ask questions. She's very determined and always wants to try new things. One day, Piper finds a mysterious egg and decides to take it home with her. She soon discovers that the egg is from an alien creature. When the egg hatches, a small alien creature called a Gleep comes out. Piper is excited to have a new friend, but she quickly learns that the Gleep is very mischievous. The Gleep loves to play tricks on Piper and get her into trouble. Piper gets frustrated with the Gleep, but she eventually learns to be patient with him. In the end, Piper and the Gleep become good friends.
