The story is about a 3 year old boy named Benjamin who is kind and helpful. He always goes out of his way to help others, even if they don't really need it. One day, he sees a little girl who is crying. He goes over to her and asks her what's wrong. She tells him that she lost her teddy bear. Benjamin offers to help her find it. He looks all around, but he can't find it. He asks the girl if she has any other ideas about where it could be. She tells him that she last saw it at the park. Benjamin goes to the park and looks for the teddy bear. He finally finds it under a bench. He gives it back to the girl and she is very happy.
