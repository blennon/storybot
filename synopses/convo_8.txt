Declan is a happy one year old boy who loves to play. He is always laughing and making everyone around him smile. One day, Declan's mommy and daddy take him to the park to play. Declan has so much fun running and playing games with his parents. When it's time to go home, Declan is so grateful for the wonderful day he had. He knows that he is loved and that he is lucky to have such great parents. This story teaches Declan (and all children) the importance of gratitude.
