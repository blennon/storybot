Evelyn is a 3 year old girl who loves to explore. She is brave and adventurous, and loves to go to the zoo. One day, when she is at the zoo with her family, she sees a lion in its enclosure. She is scared at first, but then she remember what her family has taught her about courage. She decides to be brave and go up to the lion. The lion is friendly and they become friends. Evelyn learns that being brave doesn't mean that you're never scared, it just means that you face your fears.
