Luke is a four year old boy who is very active and curious. He loves to play outside and ask a lot of questions. He is also very honest, always telling the truth even if it's something he knows he shouldn't say.

One day, Luke's curiosity gets the best of him and he asks his mom a question that he knows he shouldn't. His mom gets very upset with him and tells him that he should always be honest.

Luke is very sorry for what he did and decides to make things right. He comes up with a plan to apologize to his mom and show her how much he loves her.

With the help of his friends, Luke carries out his plan and his mom is very happy. She learns that honesty is the best policy and that her son is a very special boy.
