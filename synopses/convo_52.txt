Harper is a 3-year-old girl who loves space and all things out of this world. She lives on a planet that is always filled with sunshine and she loves to play outside with her friends. One day, Harper's friend comes to her and tells her about a new planet that they found. Harper is excited to go check it out and she asks her friend if she can come along. When they get to the new planet, they realize that it is very different from their own. There is no sun and the sky is always dark. Harper and her friend are scared at first, but then they meet some friendly aliens who show them around. The aliens teach Harper and her friend about the importance of being kind and helpful, even when things are tough. In the end, Harper and her friend are able to help the aliens fix their spaceship and they all return home safely.
