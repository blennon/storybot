Rowan is a two-year-old boy who loves to explore and ask questions. He is always on the move, and loves to discover new things. One day, while Rowan is exploring his backyard, he finds a small, injured bird. Rowan carefully picks up the bird and takes it inside to show his mom. His mom helps him nurse the bird back to health, and Rowan learns the importance of taking care of others. He also learns about gratitude, and how even the smallest act of kindness can make a big difference.
