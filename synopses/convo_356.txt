James is a 1 year old boy who is just starting to learn about the world around him. He is curious and loves to explore, but he is also very innocent. One day, James sees a bird that has been hurt and he wants to help it. His mommy tells him that he is too little to help, but James doesn't give up. He looks for a way to help the bird and finally finds a way to make it feel better. In the end, James learns that even though he is little, he can still do big things if he tries hard enough and has a kind heart.
