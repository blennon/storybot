Jace is a two-year-old boy who attends a school for the first time. He is curious and excited to learn about all the new things in his environment. As he goes through his day, he experiences different emotions. He feels happy when he gets to play with his new friends, but he also feels frustrated when he doesn't understand a lesson. Regardless of the ups and downs, Jace is grateful for his school and all the people in it. He learns that even on the tough days, there are always things to be thankful for.
