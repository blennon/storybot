Aidan is a 1 year old boy who is very adventurous, curious and playful. He often rushes into things without thinking and as a result, he gets hurt. Aidan's conflict is that he doesn't know how to be patient. He goes on a quest to find a magical cure for his impatience. Along the way, he has to overcome various challenges that teach him the importance of being patient. By the end of the story, Aidan learns that it's important to be patient and to think things through before he does them.
