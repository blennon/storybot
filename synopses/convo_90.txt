Piper is a 3 year old girl who loves to play with her toys. Sometimes she can get a little impatient, so her parents want her to learn about the importance of patience.

The story follows Piper as she tries to play with her toys, but gets frustrated when things don't go her way. She starts to understand the importance of patience when she sees how her toys work and how long it takes to do things.

In the end, Piper learns that patience is important and that she can have fun if she takes her time.
