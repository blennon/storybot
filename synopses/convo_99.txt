Declan is a young boy who lives in a kingdom near a great forest. One day, he learns that there is a dragon living in the forest and decides that he wants to find it. His parents tell him that it is too dangerous and try to dissuade him, but Declan is determined. He sets off into the forest, armed only with a sword and a shield, and begins his search. He has many adventures along the way, meeting all sorts of creatures, both friendly and not-so-friendly. But he never gives up, even when things seem hopeless. Finally, he finds the dragon's lair and faces off against the creature. Through his courage and determination, he is able to defeat the dragon and save the kingdom.
