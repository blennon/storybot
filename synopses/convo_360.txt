Ryder is a 3-year-old boy who loves to have adventures. One day, he finds a mysterious box in his backyard. When he opens it, a robot pops out and startles him. The robot introduces itself as Boxbot and says that it was sent from the future to help Ryder with his adventures.

Although at first Ryder is scared of the robot, he quickly becomes friends with it. Together, they have all sorts of fun adventures. They visit the moon, go deep sea diving, and even explore a haunted house.

Throughout their adventures, Boxbot always helps Ryder persevere when things get tough. Ryder learns that no matter what challenges he faces, he can always overcome them with perseverance. In the end, Ryder and Boxbot become best friends forever.
