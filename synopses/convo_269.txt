Kai is a three-year-old boy who loves to explore and is very honest. He goes on a treasure hunt with his friends and finds a treasure. He has to decide whether to keep it for himself or share it with his friends. He gets lost on the way home and meets some new friends. He has to decide whether to help them or not. He helps his mom bake a cake for his aunt's birthday. He accidentally drops the cake and has to decide whether to tell the truth or lie about what happened.
