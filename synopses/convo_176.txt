Aidan is a two year old boy who loves to explore and ask questions. He's very curious, which leads him to get into all sorts of trouble. One day, Aidan's curiosity gets the best of him and he finds himself in a spaceship that takes him to a faraway planet.

Aidan meets a friendly alien who helps him learn about the planet and its inhabitants. However, Aidan soon discovers that there is a evil space creature who is terrorizing the planet. With the help of his new friend, Aidan must find the courage to stand up to the space creature and save the planet.
