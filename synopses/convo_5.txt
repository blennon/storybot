Owen is a young boy who goes on a treasure hunt with his friends. They have to search for buried treasure and along the way, they learn the importance of being honest with each other. Owen's friend Sarah finds a shiny necklace and wants to keep it for herself, but Owen knows that they should share the treasure they find equally. He bravely speaks up and tells Sarah that it's not fair for her to keep the necklace all to herself. Sarah agrees and they decide to share the treasure they find from then on. In the end, they find the buried treasure and everyone is happy!
