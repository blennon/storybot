Declan is a four year old boy who loves to explore. One day, he finds a secret cave while out playing with his dog, Benny. He decides to keep it a secret and starts exploring it every day, with Benny always by his side.

One day, Declan finds a treasure deep in the cave. He's not sure what it is, but he knows it's special. He decides to take it home to show his mom.

When he gets home, Declan's mom is very excited to see the treasure. She tells him it's an ancient artifact and it's very valuable. Declan is excited to have found such a special thing and is proud of his secret cave.
