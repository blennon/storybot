Benjamin is a happy two year old who loves to play. One day, he goes to daycare and meets a bully. The bully picks on Benjamin and doesn't let him play with the other kids. Benjamin tries to stand up for himself, but the bully is too strong. Eventually, the bully knocks Benjamin down and Benjamin starts to cry. The other kids come to comfort Benjamin and the bully realizes what he's done. He feels bad and helps Benjamin up. Benjamin forgives the bully and they become friends. The story ends with a happy ending and teaches the lesson of not giving up and forgiveness.
