Piper is a 1-year-old girl who loves to explore. She's very adventurous and always up for a new adventure. One day, Piper finds herself on a water planet that she's never seen before. She meets a friendly alien who helps her find her way around. Piper soon learns that the alien is being chased by a monster and she decides to help him. With Piper's help, the alien is able to escape and Piper learns the importance of being kind.
