Willow is a young girl who finds herself lost in the forest. She begins to panic but then calms herself down and starts to think about how to find her way home. She comes across a river and decides to follow it. After walking for a while, she hears someone calling her name. It turns out to be her dog, who had followed her into the forest. Willow is relieved and happy to be reunited with her dog. They find their way home together.
