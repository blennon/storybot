"Savannah and the Magic of Gratitude" is a heartwarming story about a young girl who learns the power of gratitude. When Savannah's parents forget to pack her favorite toy for a trip, she's upset at first. But with the help of a kind stranger, she learns to see the beauty in what she does have and ends up having the best vacation ever. Along the way, she also learns that it's always better to express gratitude than to hold onto anger and resentment. By the end of the story, Savannah is a happy and grateful little girl who knows the importance of always being thankful.
