Quinn is a two year old girl who is brave and determined. She loves to explore her world and is always up for a challenge. One day, Quinn is playing in her usual spot in the park when she comes across a new toy. She is determined to get the toy, but finds that it is just out of her reach. She tries again and again, but no matter how hard she tries, she just can't seem to get it. She keeps trying until she finally succeeds. She is very happy to have gotten the toy and learns that persistence pays off.
