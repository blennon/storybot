Elizabeth is a kind, animal-loving two-year-old girl. One day, she finds a lost puppy on the street and decides to take him home. She names him Freddy and gives him a big hug. Elizabeth's mom is not too happy about the situation, but Elizabeth is determined to keep Freddy and take care of him. With a little help from her mom, Elizabeth learns how to take care of Freddy and ends up teaching him a few tricks. In the end, Elizabeth learns the importance of being kind to animals and Freddy becomes a part of the family.
